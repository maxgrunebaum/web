Sematary's discography

  • Grave House
  • Rainbow Bridge
  • Rainbow Bridge 2
  • Hundred Acre Wrist Hosted by Dj Sorrow
  • Teammate showcase

Grave House

Grave House is Sematary's first full album, containing his more simple beats and lyrics. It also contains a wide variety of samples, for example an operatic sample on Stand Your Ground. This album shows Sematary's potential as an artist.

Rainbow Bridge

Rainbow Bridge was the first release in a series of soon to be 3 albums. the productions quality of Sematary's beats as well as the complexity are much more increased, and the themes are much more specified. It also features the first God's Wisdom feature.

Rainbow Bridge 2

Rainbow Bridge 2 really shows off Sematary's music style as a whole. Not only has it come in full, but he shows that it can become even more complex and experimental, by adding singing sections and violins. Judging from leaks from RB3, it looks like his music style will become even more complex, but also have a much more refined sound, that is equally as unpalatable.

Hundred Acre Wrist Hosted by Dj Sorrow

Hunred Acre Wrist Hosted by Dj Sorrow is a very different sound from Sematary's normal, self produced and Ghost Mountain produced sounds. While the beats are much more refined, the vocals are much more distorted, with songs like "Louisville Slugger" featuring very up-pitched vocals in the intro, which is unlike what we have heard before. Hundred Acre Wrist is a nice break between Rainbow Bridge 2 and 3, which will hopefully be even more sonically overwhelming.
